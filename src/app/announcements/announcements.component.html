Announcements / Upcoming meetings

  • Restricted Parking March 2022

    In March 2022, VDOT will be restricting parking on Main Street due to a scheduled detour forcing traffic to come out from North River Rd onto S. Main Street. The exact start date of the parking restrictions or specific affected area on Main Street is not known yet. Residents will be notified as soon as VDOT provides more information. This detour is supposed to be in place for approximately one year. If you have questions, please call (540) 421-0856 or email townofmountcrawford@gmail.com

  • Water/Sewer/Trash Credit

    Due to an ARPA Federal fund received, the Town Council gave all water, sewer, trash account holders $100 towards their accounts if they were paid in full as of 12/1/2021. If your account balance on your bill is in parentheses,you have a credit balance with nothing due.
