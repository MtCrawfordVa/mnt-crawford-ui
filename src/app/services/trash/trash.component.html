Trash Services

Household Trash

  • All residents in Mt. Crawford are serviced by Mt. Crawford's contract with Waste Management.
  • Trash collection is weekly on Fridays
  • In inclement weather (snow, etc), trash will be collected the next working day
  • Billing for trash services are included in the monthly water/sewer bill. The current rate is $12.50 per month.

To request a Waste Management issued trash can or if you have any questions pertaining to trash collection, please email townofmountcrawford@gmail.com
